📖 Discord Sharing Guide
  1. Click Open Projector above — your streaming app opens a resizable window showing your scene.
  2. In Discord, join a voice channel and click Screen → Window, then pick the projector window.
  3. To capture Discord audio into the stream: on Windows click ↻ Apps and pick Discord, or Scan Devices and select the output device Discord uses, then add the source.
  4. Set monitoring to Monitor Only (mute output) so commentary goes into the stream but doesn't echo back.
